Detailed Description

A Customer is a client of a VideoStore who can rent items.

A client is identified by a unique name. At any given time a Customer has a list of items currently rented, and a balance representing rental charges, late fees, or credits (where a negative balance indicates a credit). Balances are in cents. Ordinary customers are not allowed to rent new items if they have any items overdue.
